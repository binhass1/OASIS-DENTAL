Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome, though we strongly recommend booking in advance to guarantee a time slot and minimise your wait. Emergencies are always prioritised.

I'm nervous about dental visits. Can you help?

Absolutely. Many of our patients come to us specifically because of dental anxiety. We take a gentle, communicative approach — explaining each step, pacing treatment to your comfort, and using modern anaesthesia for pain-free care.

How much does a consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary depending on the complexity of your case and any imaging required. We always provide a clear, itemised quote before any treatment begins — no surprises, ever. Call us for current pricing.

Do you treat children?

Yes — we love seeing young patients. Our pediatric dental services are designed to make children feel safe and even excited about dental visits, building healthy habits that last a lifetime.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call us immediately. We reserve same-day slots for emergencies including severe pain, swelling, broken teeth, and dental trauma. The sooner you call, the sooner we can relieve your pain and protect your tooth.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money, and major bank cards. For larger treatment plans, we can discuss staged payment scheduling — just ask our front desk during your consultation.
